Plant Mastery - A Gamified Plant Care App Prototype

This was a school project for DME-125: User Experience Fundamentals that was further iterated on in DME-211: User Experience Research and Testing which taught the core principles of UX design and exposed the students to the beginning-to-end design process.

Problem Statement - People who own houseplants sometimes have a difficult time taking care of plants because of things like lack of motivation, feeling like it’s just another task, or not knowing what the correct care is.

Possible Solution - This app aims to make caring for plants fun and informative while receiving both in-game and real-life rewards for their efforts. The more effort they put into this care, the closer they are to Plant Mastery.

Project Duration - Fall Term 2023 to Spring Term 2024

Tools Used - Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

For this project, I followed the design process Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test

Empathize - Where I conducted User Interviews, User Research, and Competitive Analysis

Define - Where I created Personas, designed an Empathy Map, and created a Journey Map

Ideate - I created User Flows and created a Site Map for Information Architecture

Prototype - Where I created Paper Wireframes, Low-fidelity Mockups, and High-Fidelity Mockups

Test - I conducted Usability Testing and Implemented Feedback

Clayton Farm & Community Market - Responsive Website and Native Mobile App Prototype

This project was developed as a capstone for the WEB-289: Internet Technologies Project course, where I applied a comprehensive range of design techniques to create a solution for the Clayton Farm & Community Market. The endeavor involved a complete website redesign on a user/upkeep-friendly platform and a native mobile app prototype.

Problem Statement - Community members who seek to support local farmers and artisans need an engaging, easy-to-navigate digital platform because they value connecting with sustainable practices and local products but currently lack a cohesive online space that facilitates these interactions and transactions.

Possible Solution - This comprehensive website redesign aims to transform the way community members connect with local agriculture, offering a visually appealing, user-friendly interface showcases local farmers, community events, and more.

A native mobile app will introduce features like location-based services, instant notifications, and mobile payment options, creating a seamless and enriched market experience that bridges the gap between consumers and local artisans effectively.

Project Duration - Spring Term 2024

Tools Used - Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Wix Studio

For this project, I followed a design document I created to fit the criteria needed for the native mobile app and website.

Since this was a website redesign, this non-profit organization required a platform that was user-friendly for their day-to-day upkeep

Wix Studio is a powerful and user-friendly website builder where I got to showcase my ability to design a user-centric interface that would solve the problems they were facing.

The Native Mobile App was created with different features but a similar interface to the website with elements that provided brand continuity.

Niche Nest Furniture - Responsive Website, Mobile App Prototype, Animations, and More

This subject was used for two separate school projects. GRD-240: User Interface/User Experience and DME-140: Intro to Audio/Video Media. I practiced principles of branding, competitive research, Adobe XD in both courses, and learned Adobe Premier Pro and After Effects basic use in DME-140.

Problem Statement - Online Shoppers who seek unique, high-quality wooden furniture need an intuitive, responsive e-commerce platform to purchase furniture conveniently because they value a seamless and engaging shopping experience.

Possible Solution - This responsive website aims to make the process of buying elegant high-quality wooden furniture engaging and streamlined, with a user-friendly clean interface and clear company branding that ensures a trustworthy and dependable experience.

Project Duration - Spring Term 2024

Tools Used - Adobe: XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premier Pro, and After Effects

I created low and high fidelity mockups for a responsive website and mobile app prototype.

I made animated logos and microinteractions for this furniture site to introduce dynamic elements and showcase my newly learned abilities in animation software

The final project was several portions we created combined into a cumulative animated design
